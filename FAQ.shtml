FAQ

UCLA Groups

Who should get involved?

▼

  • UCLA graduate and undergraduate student groups with a STEM connection.
  • UCLA graduate students and faculty who would like to give a public talk.

What would we do at the event?

▼

  • Host a booth where you interact with the public.
  • Demonstrate scientific concepts using hands-on demonstrations and activities.
  • Setup/cleanup
  • Organize your own volunteers to man your booth.

What do we have to pay for?

▼

  • EYU provides you with the booth itself (10x10' tent, one table, two chairs, more available upon request) for free.
  • You are responsibe for buying your own supplies, but we do apply for CPC funding as an organization. You can submit a budger proposal to our fundraising coordinator and some of your supplies may be payed for.

Can we sell things at the event?

▼

Yes, but you will need to run your products by our merchandise coordinator.

How do I reserve a booth?

▼

  • Fill out a booth application form
  • Fill out a volunteer form for you and your volunteers (this gets you a t-shirt the day of EYU).
  • Send supplies budget request to fundraising coordinator by September 20th.

Non-UCLA Groups

How can we get involved?

▼

Non-UCLA groups, corporations, and individuals are asked to make a donation (either monetary or product) in order to reserve a booth at our event. Please contact our fundraising coordinator for more information.
